March 3, 2021 by Prasanna Half-Day Leave Application: Sample of Half Day Leave Application for Employees. If any person needs to get a leave for half-day he/she requires to write an application to the management. In your half-day leave the application you have to specify the reason for which you are conforming to take the leave or if it is personal. You also have to ensure the management that your half leave won’t influence the working of that day.
